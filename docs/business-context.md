# Business Context

This project simulates the analytical environment of a national company operating in both B2B and B2C channels across Brazil. Leadership needs a single decision layer to track revenue, profitability, discount pressure, regional execution, category performance, and target attainment without relying on disconnected spreadsheets.

The business sells a mixed portfolio of technology, furniture, and office supply products through online, retail, distributor, and direct sales channels. Commercial managers care not only about sales growth, but about whether that growth is translating into healthy margin, efficient discounting, and consistent target delivery.

The dashboard is designed for sales leadership, finance partners, and regional managers who need to answer a practical question: where is the company growing efficiently, and where is revenue masking margin leakage or weak commercial execution?
